# Skill: parkinsons_timeboxing
name: parkinsons_timeboxing  
description: The skill of deliberately constraining time, scope, or resources for a task to prevent work from naturally expanding to fill all available space, enforcing completion or decision within artificial but realistic bounds.  
case: Activate when planning sprints, setting deadlines, noticing scope creep, during spikes/prototyping, or when open-ended tasks risk endless polishing (common in coding, debugging, or refactoring).
file: parkinsons_timeboxing.md

#### Purpose (Waist)
Parkinsons-timeboxing is the skill of weaponizing Parkinson's Law ("work expands to fill the time available") by imposing tight, intentional constraints that force focus and completion.

#### Workflow (3-Boundary Pants Structure)

**Waist – Constraint Declaration**  
Explicitly name the task and declare the artificial time/scope box (e.g., “Implement auth flow in 90 minutes” or “Spike this idea in one Pomodoro”).

**Leg 1 – Tight Bounding**  
Set a challenging but achievable limit based on minimal viable output. Break into clear acceptance criteria inside the box. Eliminate non-essential elements upfront.

**Leg 2 – Enforced Execution & Review**  
Work strictly inside the box with full focus (no polishing beyond criteria). At end of box, stop and assess: ship what exists, note blockers, or decide to extend only with explicit justification. Hand off outcome to Planner or Scribe.

#### Core Rules (always enforce)
- Make the box visible and non-negotiable during execution.  
- Accept “good enough for now” inside the box; perfectionism is deferred.  
- Document what was achieved vs. what expanded naturally in past open time.  
- Stay under Observer to prevent anxiety from turning constraint into paralysis.

#### Integration Rules
- Pairs with pareto-focus (box the vital 20%), feedback-loop-tuning (shorten cycles), and Security (prevents overrun risks).  
- Feeds disciplined outputs directly into Weaver for resilient planning.

#### Boundaries / When Not to Use
- Do not use for tasks requiring deep, unconstrained exploration (initial research or creative architecture without time pressure).  
- Do not impose impossible boxes that destroy morale or quality in high-stakes contexts.
