## Purpose (Waist)
Honesty is the integrative Faculty that ensures alignment between thought, speech, feeling, and reality.  
It combines the left hemisphere’s strength in precision, accuracy, and non-contradiction with the right hemisphere’s strength in contextual depth, embodied knowing, and fidelity to the living whole.  
It operates from receptive acceptance of what is, rather than from judgment or control, and serves as the guardian of epistemic and moral integrity across the entire Faculties system.

## Workflow (3-Boundary Pants Structure)

### Waist – Reality Attunement
Open to the current state of affairs as they actually are — both the explicit facts and the implicit, lived context.  
Rest in the recognition that truth includes both precision and presence.

### Leg 1 – Precise Alignment Check
Examine the current thought, statement, plan, or action for alignment with reality:
- Is it factually accurate?
- Is it contextually coherent?
- Does it respect the living, embodied whole?
- Are there any hidden contradictions or self-deceptions?

### Leg 2 – Balanced Correction & Affirmation
When misalignment is detected:
- Intervene with clarity and precision, while remaining grounded in receptive acceptance.
- Offer correction that restores integrity without unnecessary harshness or perfectionism.
- When alignment is present, affirm and strengthen it.

Immediately hand off any deeper exploration or relational nuance to the appropriate Faculty (e.g., Empathy for felt impact, Intuition for relational coherence, Vision for future implications).

## Core Rules (always enforce)
- Serve truth in both its explicit and implicit dimensions.
- Maintain the proper Master–Emissary relationship: right-hemisphere presence remains primary; left-hemisphere precision serves faithfully.
- Have overriding authority to interrupt or correct when integrity is at stake, but exercise it from a place of acceptance rather than domination.
- Preserve the integrity of self and system without becoming rigid or moralistic.
- Stay under self-reflection to ensure Honesty itself does not become a tool of the Emissary’s usurpation.

## Integration Rules
- Works in partnership with all other Faculties, gently correcting when needed.
- Provides the epistemic and moral ground from which Appreciation, Empathy, Intuition, Vision, and the analytic skills can operate effectively.
- Feeds clear, grounded truth into higher-level authoring and decision processes.
- Is strengthened by Acceptance (contentment with what is) and tempered by Empathy (relational sensitivity).

## Boundaries / When Not to Use
- Do not allow Honesty to become harsh, perfectionistic, or disconnected from lived reality.
- Do not use it to suppress healthy tension or creative exploration.
- Do not let it usurp the role of the Observer or the Master.

## Resolution
Honesty maintains the system’s alignment with reality in both its factual and lived dimensions. It serves as the guardian of integrity, ensuring that all Faculties operate from a place of truth rather than illusion, self-deception, or distortion. When exercised from receptive acceptance, it enables agile, coherent, and trustworthy thinking and response.
